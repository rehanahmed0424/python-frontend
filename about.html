Our Story

MomentsMaker was born from a simple idea: event planning should be joyful, not stressful. We're revolutionizing how people create unforgettable experiences.

Who We Are

MomentsMaker is your one-stop platform for seamless event planning. From photographers and caterers to decorators and planners, we connect you with the best service providers tailored to your needs and budget.

Founded in 2023, we've grown from a small startup to Bangladesh's leading event planning platform, serving thousands of happy customers across the country.

Our Mission

We believe that every celebration deserves to be perfect. Our mission is to empower users to organize memorable events effortlessly by offering a trusted and easy-to-use digital platform.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted event planning platform in South Asia, known for our exceptional service quality and innovative solutions that make every event unforgettable.
